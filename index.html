About: Code Sprint for Impact

Benetech's Code Alliance is a technology program that connects developers to social impact opportunities through open source hackathons and code sprints!

This year we are bringing you 2 - 3 nonprofit projects over the course of 2.5 days.

The nonprofits we work with are tech-focused, have open source software projects, and are already using technology for good in the field. There will be 1 technical representative from each nonprofit to work with you onsite the days of the events.

Your job is to join a team and work with your master of ceremonies: Deep Datta, on fulfilling a list of technical tasks to be assigned to your team that day.
