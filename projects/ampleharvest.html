AmpleHarvest.org helps 42 million home and community gardeners end food waste and hunger by educating and enabling them to donate their excess garden produce to one of 8,068 nearby food pantries across America.

Project Details


Read our full project notes here

We will be updating the donation strategy for AmpleHarvest.org including changing the design, user experience, and data model for the page. We will also focus on digital marketing: Facebook, SEO, and Adwords.

We will also be building in new capabilities including a mobile image capture feature, and capturing data from users who are engaging with our food pantry lookup system.

We have a large number of people coming to our site, and a significant number coming to the donation page, but the conversion rate is very low. For example, a static PSA appeared in 2016 4.8 billion times. Donations from the page were under 100 times. We need a complete look at and overhaul of the donation process. The site is coded in Wordpress and we’re not yet in a positon to do a site rebuild – something we’d very much like to do (already spec’ed out) but our first critical need is better data around the process.
